Botswana 🇧🇼

Botswana is a landlocked country in Southern Africa known for its beautiful landscapes, wildlife and rich Setswana culture.

Language

Setswana is the national language of Botswana. Common greetings include Dumela (Hello) and Ke a leboga (Thank you).

Traditional Food

Popular foods include Seswaa, Bogobe and Morogo.

Traditional Dance

Setapa and Phathisi are popular traditional dances.

Wedding Ceremonies

Bogadi (bride price) is an important part of Botswana weddings.

✨ But That's Not All...

One of the most fascinating things about Botswana is its breathtaking tourism. From the magnificent Okavango Delta to the vast Kalahari Desert, Botswana is truly a hidden gem of Africa.

The Okavango Delta is one of the world's largest inland deltas and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 🐘🦁
