About This Website

Learn about Botswana and its beautiful traditions

I created this website because I wanted to bring our culture out there for the world to see, just as we are able to learn about other cultures.I believe people should also get the chance to learn about ours.

I am from Serowe, a town in the Central District of Botswana. Growing up there, I have always been proud of where I come from. Botswana is just a peaceful country- I wouldn't have it any other way!

What I love most about Botswana is the people and the country in general. There is just something special about this place that words cannot fully describe. From the culture , to the traditions, to the warmth of the people.

About Botswana

Botswana is known for the Okavango Delta, the Kalahari Desert and its wildlife. It is also known for being one of the most stable and peaceful countries in Africa.

The people of Botswana are called Batswana and we speak Setswana as our national language.

I hope that this website helps people learn more about African traditions and appreciate the beauty of African culture.
