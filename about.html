About This Website

I created this website because I wanted to share some of the amazing traditions and customs that come from Africa.

I am from Botswana, which is a country in Southern Africa. Growing up, I have always been proud of our culture and traditions. I think it is important to learn about where we come from.

Botswana has its own rich culture too. The Setswana language and traditions are a big part of who we are as a people.

About Botswana

Botswana is known for the Okavango Delta, the Kalahari Desert and its wildlife. It is also known for being one of the most stable and peaceful countries in Africa.

The people of Botswana are called Batswana and we speak Setswana as our national language.

I hope that this website helps people learn more about African traditions and appreciate the beauty of African culture.
