Welcome to African Traditions

Africa is a continent with a very rich history and culture. There are so many different traditions and customs that have been passed down for generations.

This website looks at some of those traditions and why they are still important today.

Africa has 54 countries and hundreds of ethnic groups, each with their own unique customs and way of life.

Did You Know?

Africa is the second largest continent in the world and has over 2000 different languages spoken across its countries.

Some African Traditions

Music & Dance

Music and dance are a big part of African culture. They are used in celebrations, ceremonies and everyday life.

Traditional Food

Every African country has its own traditional food. Food is often shared with family and community as a sign of unity.

Traditional Clothing

African traditional clothing is colourful and meaningful. Many outfits are worn during special occasions and ceremonies.
